Circular Economy

One of the focus areas that is relevant in the consumption based economy is ‘Waste Management’ and migration towards a sustainable ‘Circular Economy’ through the use of 3R - Recovery, Reuse & Recycle for ‘Waste to Wealth’(monetization) translation.

We leverage IoT, data analytics & automation to deliver solutions that can be integrated with the existing infrastructure for holistic upgrade to address the following challenges/opportunities:

  • Segregation at Source
  • Waste to Energy
  • Electronic Waste
  • Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR)

Climate change

Non-toxic biodegradable waste ends up in landfills (dumping grounds) owing to lack of composting venues or awareness. On decomposition, this waste releases Greenhouse Gases(GHGs) that lead to global warming & subsequent climate change.
Similarly, non-toxic medical waste also finds itself in landfills without having subsequent value derived from it.
At Zargun ES, we are developing smart solutions that can augments players that are focused

  • Non-toxic Biodegradable waste (feedstock for composting)
  • Non-toxic Medical waste (feedstock for recycling)
